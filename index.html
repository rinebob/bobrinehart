Tryppio

Re-imagined Google Earth Panoramio Layer

Travel related social media website

www.tryppio.com
  • Recreation of Google Earth ‘Panoramio’ layer with new extensions.
  • Enables travelers to find / research travel destinations and meet local businesses, guides and experts.
  • Users upload geo-located images and create profiles for others to search. Search feature returns database of images displayed on a Google Map.
  • User can click an image to learn more or open a profile of the creator. Travelers can chat real- time with locals to meet, book reservations, create trip plans etc.
  • Revenue opportunity through monthly fee from locals for maintaining profiles and advertising.
